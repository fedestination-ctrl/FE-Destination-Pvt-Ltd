Study in Europe 🇪🇺

🎓 Entry Requirements

  • High School or Bachelor’s Degree depending on the program level.
  • IELTS (6.0+ bands) or alternative English Proficiency Proof / Medium of Instruction (MOI).
  • Blocked Account / Financial Proof as per country regulations.
  • Motivational Letter & Academic CV.

🏙️ Famous Study Hubs

Germany, France, Italy, and Ireland offer world-class public universities with low or zero tuition fees, making Europe a top destination for international students.
