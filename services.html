Language Test Preparation – IELTS, PTE, German A1

IELTS: One of the world’s most well-known English language competency exams is the International English Language Testing System (IELTS), which is widely recognized by colleges, companies, and immigration agencies in English-speaking nations.

Types of IELTS

IELTS Academic: For applicants seeking professional registration or higher education in English-speaking countries.

IELTS General Training: Ideal for secondary school, job seekers, or immigration applicants moving to English-speaking countries.
